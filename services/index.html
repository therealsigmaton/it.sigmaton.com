How engagements work

Most engagements start with a short discovery to confirm scope, stakeholders, risks, and the success criteria. From there we execute via milestones (or delivery sprints), with documentation and handover built into the plan.

  • Discovery: current state, objectives, constraints, risk register, scope confirmation
  • Plan: milestones, RACI, governance cadence, acceptance criteria
  • Delivery: execution + status reporting + issue/risk management
  • Validation: QA/UAT support, controls evidence, performance checks
  • Handover: runbooks, training, documentation, operational readiness
